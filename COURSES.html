Learn a Foreign Language

Our methodology is based on a single parameter: the confidence to communicate effectively in the language within the first 10 lessons. Our hands-on approach is based on years of R&D conducted across India and overseas. Learning a language is intrinsically woven into the history, culture, and literature of the country's native speakers and we ensure that you experience all of these aspects during the course.

About Apriori Education

We believe in delivering quality and are unwavering in our commitment to achieve the benchmarks of excellence every single time. Our pedagogy is based on more than 15 years of expertise across various verticals, such as International Standardised Tests, Corporate Training Modules, and Foreign Languages.

Foundation Course

The Foundation Course corresponds to the A1.1 of the CEFR, also referred to as the pre-beginner or introductory level. During the course, we prioritize speaking skills and listening comprehension over written communication and reading comprehension.

Intermediate Course

The Intermediate Course, in conjunction with the Foundation Course, traverses through the first three levels of aptitude equivalent to A1, A2, and B1 levels. The first two levels cover all the basic aspects of the language and the third focusses on making the student confident enough to express herself/ himself in a professional setting.

Advanced Course

This course is designed for students who wish to attain mastery of the language and become proficient. Most students who opt for this course wish to study the language at the UG level. The students are able to clear the test for the C1 level with a few weeks of study and practice, which is often a requirement for the UG courses in the host country.
